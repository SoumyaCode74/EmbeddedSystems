*****Read from heater and display temperature on LCD. Alarm if temperature is above/below a specified limit with message*****

Statement: Write a program to continuously read the temperature from the thermal sensor. Display the temperature on LCD in Kelvin in first line of a 16x2 DTM LCD.

Conditions: 
1) When temperature is above a setvalue, display HIGH TEMPERATURE on second line. 
2) When temperature is within 10 counts of maximum preset temperature, sound the alarm with frequency 0.5 Hz and display OVERLOAD!. Send heater shutdown signal.
3) When temperature is below a setvalue, display LOW TEMPERATURE on second line. Send heater start signal.
4) Assign RB0 as a manual start button of latch NO type, assign RB1 as a manual stop button of latching NC type.
